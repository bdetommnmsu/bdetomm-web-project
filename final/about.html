About GreenTech Solutions

Founded on the belief that sustainable technology should be accessible to every business — not just the Fortune 500.

Our Story

GreenTech Solutions was founded in 2018 by a team of environmental engineers and software developers who shared a frustrating observation: the tools to fight climate change existed, but they were locked behind enterprise price tags that small and medium businesses simply couldn't afford.

We set out to change that. Starting with a single energy monitoring device built in a garage in Santa Fe, New Mexico, we've grown into a full suite of eco-friendly technology solutions trusted by over 500 businesses across North America.

Today, our mission remains exactly what it was on day one: make sustainability practical, affordable, and measurable for every business that wants to do better for the planet.

Our Values

Everything we build and every decision we make is guided by these core principles.

🌎

Planet First

Every product decision starts with one question: is this better for the environment? If the answer isn't yes, we go back to the drawing board.

🤝

Accessibility

Sustainability shouldn't be a luxury. We price our products fairly and offer flexible payment plans so any business can afford to go green.

📊

Transparency

We publish our own carbon footprint annually and hold ourselves to the same standards we ask of our clients. No greenwashing, ever.

💡

Innovation

We invest 20% of revenue back into R&D every year, ensuring our clients always have access to the most advanced eco-tech available.

Our Journey

Key milestones that shaped GreenTech Solutions into what it is today.

2018

Founded in Santa Fe

Maya Chen and James Okafor launch GreenTech from a garage with a single energy monitoring prototype.

2019

First 50 Clients

EcoCharge Pro launches commercially. The team grows to 8 people and signs first 50 paying business clients.

2020

CarbonTrack Software

GreenTech releases its flagship carbon tracking SaaS platform, filling a critical gap for SMB compliance reporting.

2021

Series A Funding

Raises $4.2M in Series A funding to expand the product line and open offices in Austin and Denver.

2022

SmartSort 3000 Launch

AI-powered recycling system launches to widespread acclaim, winning the EPA Small Business Innovation Award.

2024

500 Clients Milestone

GreenTech surpasses 500 active business clients and expands to a fourth office location in Portland, Oregon.

2026

Next Generation Platform

GreenTech launches its unified sustainability platform, integrating all products into one seamless dashboard.
