Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does setup take?

Most hardware products are plug-and-play and can be fully operational within a single business day. Our software products are cloud-based and ready immediately after signup.

Do you offer financing?

Yes - we offer 12, 24, and 36-month payment plans on all hardware products. Many of our hardware products also qualify for federal and state green energy tax credits.

Is there a free trial?

All software plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. We also offer free 30-minute consultations for businesses exploring our full product suite.

What support do you offer?

Every client gets a dedicated sustainability consultant plus access to our email and phone support team Monday through Friday, 8am–5pm MT.

Are your products certified?

All GreenTech hardware products are Energy Star certified and meet EPA standards. Our company is also ISO 14001 and B Corp certified.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes - we ship hardware products to all 50 US states. We also have regional offices in Santa Fe, Austin, Denver, and Portland for in-person consultations.
