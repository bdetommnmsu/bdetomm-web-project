Quick Travel Tips

Wherever you end up, keep these in mind:

  1. Research visa requirements at least 3 months before departure
  2. Always purchase travel insurance - it's worth every penny
  3. Learn a few words in the local language - locals love the effort
  4. Download offline maps before you land
  5. Pack light - you'll thank yourself later
